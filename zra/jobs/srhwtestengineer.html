Senior Hardware Test Engineer

Full-time Position • Redwood City, CA

Apply Now

At Mayfield Robotics, we’re building an adorable home robot. Our team, based out of Redwood City, CA, is growing quickly and we need some help to get our robot designed, built, and shipped.

Role

We need a seasoned and versatile engineer to ensure that our army of robots are ready to be deployed in customer homes. You will use your motivation and ability to plan, prepare, and execute tests on systems and components, and then put those results to good use. Robot guts are messy and complicated, chock full of wires, actuators and circuits, with degrees of freedom everywhere you look. Therefore, you must enjoy tinkering with complex electro-mechanical systems from the component level all the way up to testing fully integrated systems. While you’re at it, it would be great if you could document this stuff along the way.

Responsibilities

  • Design, execute, and document tests (formal and informal reports)
  • Perform testing for development, verification and validation
  • Evaluate components, subsystems, and full systems
  • Don’t waste time: automate whenever possible with scripts and fixtures
  • Analyze data and make recommendations on unusual or difficult technical problems
  • Verify that the robots comply with the three laws of robotics, and other regulations as appropriate
  • Experience with ISO, ERP/MRP systems, and industry experience is strongly preferred
  • Occasional travel to contract manufacturers and vendors in far-flung locations

Qualifications

  • BS in engineering
  • Solid understanding of fundamental engineering principles
  • Versatility mandatory. In addition to having an intimate knowledge of core engineering fields, you understand how all the pieces fit together into integrated systems, and how they impact performance.
  • A strong sense of ownership over everything you ship; you wouldn’t call something "done" until you’re confident it’s correct.
  • Understand requirements beyond just the spec, and how your technical contributions will influence the success of future products
  • Expertise with instrumentation, data acquisition, emulation, simulation and modeling tools
  • Familiarity with regulatory testing (FCC, ISTA, CE, etc.) preferred
  • 7 years of working on hard engineering problems, and shipping product
  • Capable of solving complex problems with little to no supervision on schedule as an individual or as a member of an integrated team
  • Dangerous hobbies a plus

How to Apply

To apply, send your resume to jobs+srhwtestengineer@mayfieldrobotics.com.
