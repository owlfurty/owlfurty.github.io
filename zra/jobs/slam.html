Robotics SLAM Engineer

Full-time Position • Redwood City, CA

Apply Now

Mayfield Robotics is creating an adorable robot for the home. Our team, based in Redwood City, CA, is growing quickly and we need some help to get our robot designed, built, and shipped. We are looking for a Robotics SLAM Engineer who is passionate about bringing robots to the consumer market. Our ideal candidate is someone who has substantial experience with SLAM, particularly within home or other indoor environments. If you’re looking for a position working on robots that will interact with people in their homes, this is the job for you.

Role

You would be responsible for the development of a robust SLAM system for an indoor robot that will operate in consumer homes. You will help design, develop, and deploy a robust localization and mapping system for a home robot, including developing techniques for lifelong mapping and global localization.

Requirements

  • Experience with SLAM, pose estimation, probabilistic filtering, sensor fusion
  • Demonstrated experience with development on physical robots
  • Strong C++ and python skills (3+ years of experience necessary)
  • Strong system integration skills
  • Extensive experience with running code on actual robots
  • MS degree or a BS with 5 years of relevant experience in Computer Science or a related field

Nice to Have, but not Required

  • ROS experience
  • Experience on embedded platforms
  • Experience with visual SLAM
  • Ph.D in relevant field

How to Apply

To apply, send your resume to jobs+slam@mayfieldrobotics.com.
