Lead Hardware Engineer

Full-time Position • Redwood City, CA

Apply Now

Mayfield Robotics is creating an adorable robot for the home. We are looking for a Lead Hardware Engineer to lead our team of firmware, electrical, mechanical, and mechatronics engineers.

Role

In this role, you will help guide our efforts with an eye towards manufacturability, while keeping in mind the needs of the entire company. We are looking for a leader who understands how electronics, mechanics, optics, firmware, and software apps come together to create a complete product. We work in a dynamic environment and are working to successfully deliver a new type of robot to consumers under tight deadlines and cost constraints. We are looking for someone who is enthusiastic and happy to roll up his/her sleeves and solve day-to-day issues, while also having the ability to plan ahead.

You will be expected to:

  • Directly manage our hardware team of 5-9 people
  • Be responsible for project management and meeting of milestone goals
  • Foster a culture of creativity and problem solving
  • Oversee the day-to-day creation of custom sensors, processor boards, charging solutions, compliance testing, Wi-Fi hardware, audio solutions, cameras, and more
  • As Mayfield scales, you’ll implement and optimize our various hardware development and manufacturing pipelines – keeping our organization efficient, nimble and creative

Desired Experience and Expertise

  • 10+ years of experience shipping reliable and appealing products in the consumer electronic, toy, robotic, or medical solution space
  • Proven ability to lead the team in making effective compromises among good, fast, cheap, and other product variables
  • Understanding of the integration of complex subsystems into a cost-engineered consumer product
  • Experience with high-volume consumer product manufacturing processes
  • Proven leadership experience and effective communications skills
  • ME or EE degree (Masters degree preferred but experience is most important)
  • Disciplined project management skills and experience with agile development practices
  • Broad knowledge of and experience with prototyping processes
  • Experience building a tech pipeline for new products
  • Knowledge of product safety and regulatory standards (US needed; global preferred), product conformance regulations such as MPG, CE, FCC, etc.
  • Experience interfacing with manufacturing partners and vendors
  • Understanding of the issues common to electromechanical products, especially relative to moving mechanisms

Nice-to-Haves

  • Start-up experience
  • PCB design experience

How to Apply

To apply, send your resume to jobs+leadhardware@mayfieldrobotics.com.
