Visual Product Designer

Full-time Position • Redwood City, CA

Apply Now

If you’ve ever loved Furby or R2D2, do we have a job for you. This is your chance to help shape the next great bot. At Mayfield Robotics, we’re building an adorable home robot. Our small team is compiled of roboticist PhDs, software engineers, designers and one is even a former voice-over actress! We are based out of Redwood City and things are moving fast here, so we need your help to get our robot designed, built, and shipped.

Who You Are

We are looking for a visual designer with generalist leanings to join our small but feisty Product Team. This key role (because every role is key here at our startup) will help design and launch our new home robot to the world. We’re creating a new brand in a new category with a new kind of product.

What You’ll Do

You’ll work closely with the VP of Product, UX Lead, and Design Technologist (and our partners in engineering and development) on the design of not only the app that accompanies our robot, but also the marketing, packaging, collateral, and even on the robot itself, helping to define and refine our visual identity across all touch points and platforms, including hardware, the web, and mobile apps.

But wait, there’s more! Because everyone here wears multiple hats, you’ll also be involved in product strategy, user research (in the field), human-robot interaction, marketing strategy, and much, much more, all leading up to the launch of an adorable home robot.

Specifically, you’ll work on:

  • the UI of the iOS and Android apps that allow users to control the robot and learn about what the robot is up to
  • the UI of the website where users will learn about and purchase the robot marketing material for CES and other events
  • designing and laying out collateral and packaging for the robot, including the start up booklet
  • expanding and refine the brand of Mayfield and the individual brand of the robot
  • visual moments on the robot itself
  • how the robot works, moves, and acts and how it relates to people
  • talking to and observing users on all of the above

Details:

  • Full-time, on-site job in Redwood City (no remote work)
  • Full benefits including: health/dental/vision, paid holidays, gym membership, laundry/dry cleaning and 401k
  • Pay is competitive

How to Apply

If this all sounds interesting to you and is a good match for your skills, send a note to jobs+visualdesign@mayfieldrobotics.com with a resume and a link to your portfolio.

Mayfield Robotics is an Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action Employer committed to diversity in the workplace. M/F/V/D
