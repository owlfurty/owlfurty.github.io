Robot DevOps Engineer

Full-time Position • Redwood City, CA

Apply Now

Mayfield Robotics is creating an adorable robot for the home. Our team, based in Redwood City, CA is looking for a Robot DevOps Engineer who is excited to help us develop and improve our development process.

Role

As part of our team, you will be the person we look to for recommendations on how to build up the rest of our software stack. You will also be responsible for maintaining our current continuous integration and build pipelines, as well as developing new pipelines, scripts, and frameworks for continuous integration, build (of both software and robots), test (of software, low-level hardware components, and high-level robot functionality), and deployment. Additionally, you will need to provide occasional sysadmin help, including debugging and maintaining our network infrastructure (for both humans and robots).

Requirements

  • Experience with Linux shell scripting
  • C/C++ and Python familiarity
  • Linux PC and embedded Linux software development
  • ARM processors
  • Experience with Travis, Jenkins, or some other CI system
  • Familiarity with Ansible, Chef or Puppet etc.
  • Experience using Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • Networking experience (subnets, routing, VPN)
  • BS degree or equivalent experience
  • At least 3 years of of real-world experience

Nice to Have, but not Required

  • Experience with ROS

How to Apply

To apply, send your resume to jobs+devops@mayfieldrobotics.com.
